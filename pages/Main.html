What is Film Productions?

Film Productions is a student-run and student-led organization that focuses on the production of narrative short films. The club’s members are responsible for every step of production from conception to distribution. Through practical experience and several leadership opportunities, Film Productions hopes to artistically and professionally enrich Cal State LA students with any amount of interest in filmmaking.

Why Should You Join Us?

If you’re a film student, education isn’t everything - there’s no better way to practice your craft than to get out there and do it. We provide a solid foundation of resources and support for you to exercise your talents outside of the classroom.

If you aren’t a film student but can’t resist the allure of show business, Film Productions is the club for you! Experienced officers and fellow club members will instruct you on your journey to become an independent filmmaker alongside them.

Whatever your interests are - whether they’re being on camera and seeing your performance on the big screen, developing skills in lighting or camerwork, writing your first screenplay, or anything else - Film Productions will provide an opportunity for you to do it. All you have to do is sign up.
