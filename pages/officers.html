Additional Officers

Additional Executives generally assist all other Officers and help maintain productivity within the club. Responsibilities may include but are not limited to: planning fundraisers and socials, being present when other Officers are unable.

Supplementary officer positions may vary on a semester-to-semester basis depending on club needs. Officers oversee a specific club function and report to Executive Officers. Applications for each semester open at the end of the semester that precedes it. Such positions may include:

  • Head of Writing
  • Head of Fundraising
  • Head of Socials
  • Head of Marketing
  • Head(s) of Production
  • Head of Equipment
  • Head of Production Design
