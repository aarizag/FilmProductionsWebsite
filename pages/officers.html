Officer Positions
For a full list of our current Officer positions and their incumbents, please contact us at FilmProductionsLA@gmail.com

  • President
  • The President of Film Productions is responsible for planning, organizing, and carrying out responsibilities associated with being the club’s chief executive officer, such as, but not limited to: appointing and delegating tasks, attending all meetings, setting goals for the club and making sure the club stays on course, solving problems within or regarding the club, and making sure each meeting is well planned and organized.
  • Vice President
  • The Vice-President of Film Productions fulfills the duties of the President when the President is absent or otherwise unable, or performs duties assigned by the President. This role is vital, in that the Vice-President and the President must always remain in good standing and in contact with each other.
  • Treasurer
  • The Treasurer establishes the organization’s budget and records any and all financial transactions. The Treasurer must generally attend all fundraiser and club events that have money involved and must consistently take inventory.
  • Additional Executive Officers (2)
  • Additional Executives generally assist all other Officers and help maintain productivity within the club. Responsibilities may include but are not limited to: planning fundraisers and socials, being present when other Officers are unable.
  • Supplementary Officers
  • Supplementary officer positions may vary on a semester-to-semester basis depending on club needs. Officers oversee a specific club function and report to Executive Officers. Applications for each semester open at the end of the semester that precedes it. Such positions may include:
    • Head of Writing
    • Head of Fundraising
    • Head of Socials
    • Head(s) of Production
    • Head of Marketing
    • Head of Production Design
    • Head of Equipment
